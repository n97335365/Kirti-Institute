About Us

Kirti Institute of Textile Designing is a highly reputed institute that provides quality training in textile designing and related skills. Established on 15 May 2018, the institute has been dedicated to helping students build a strong career in the textile industry. Here, students are trained with both practical knowledge and modern designing techniques, including the use of professional software. Over the years, Kirti Institute has successfully helped many students gain employment and become financially independent. With experienced guidance and a focus on real industry skills, the institute continues to shape a bright future for its students.
